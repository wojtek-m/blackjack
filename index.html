Rules:

(click on the rules window to close it)

  1. Aces may be counted as 1 or 11 points. J, Q and K count as 10 points.
  2. The value of a hand is the sum of the point values of the individual cards.
  3. The player whos hand value go over 21 points busts and looses.
  4. If no one busts, the higher value hand wins.
  5. Dealer wins all the draws.
  6. You can double the bet on any starting hand, but you will be forced to stand after hitting one more card.
  7. You can split any paired hand, you then play two separate hands with a bet on each (total double bet).
  8. Result of any hand can be overturn by the member of the Omerta family.
  9. You can take one more card by Hitting or go to showdown by Standing.
  10. You start with 2000 chips. The rake is 5%, so each round you win 95 or loose 100 chips.
  11. If your chip count is negative it means you are in debt to the Omertas...
  12. Never get in debt to the Omertas!
